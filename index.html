Government Schemes
for Farmers

Know your rights. These schemes can significantly boost your income.

Central
PM-KISAN
Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi gives ₹6,000 per year in three instalments directly to farmer bank accounts.
₹6,000/year direct benefit
Central
PMFBY – Crop Insurance
Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana provides financial support to farmers who suffer crop loss from natural calamities.
Full crop loss coverage
Central
Kisan Credit Card
KCC provides affordable short-term credit for cultivation expenses, maintenance and allied activities at low interest.
Loans at 4% interest
Maharashtra
Magel Tyala Shet Tale
Maharashtra scheme for farm pond construction providing irrigation support to every farmer who applies.
Free farm pond construction
Central
eNAM – Market Portal
National Agriculture Market allows farmers to sell produce online across all APMCs, getting the best price nationally.
Best nationwide price
Central
Soil Health Card
Free soil testing for every farmer with personalised fertilizer recommendations to improve productivity and reduce input costs.
Free + personalized advice
